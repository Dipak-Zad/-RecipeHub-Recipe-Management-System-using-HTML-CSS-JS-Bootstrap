Bulgur & Quinoa lunch bowls

These meal prep grain bowls use one base and two deliciously different toppings. Choose from avocado, olives and rocket or chickpeas, beetroot and orange

prep time

5 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : brunch healthy food

instructions

step 1

Tip the onion and bulgur mix into a pan, pour over 600ml water and stir in the thyme and bouillon. Cook, covered, over a low heat for 15 mins, then leave to stand for 10 mins. All the liquid should now be absorbed. When cool, remove the thyme and divide the bulgur between four bowls or plastic containers.

step 2

For the avocado topping, toss all the ingredients together except for the rocket. Pile onto two portions of the bulgur and top with the rocket.

step 3

For the beetroot topping, first pile the chickpeas on top, then toss the beetroot with the tomato, mint, cumin, a good pinch of cinnamon, the oil and vinegar. Toss well, add the orange, then pile onto the remaining portions of bulghur, scatter with the pine nuts and sprinkle with extra cinnamon. Chill in the fridge until needed.

ingredients

For the bulgur base 1 large onion , very finely chopped

For the bulgur base 150g bulgur and quinoa (this comes ready mixed)

For the bulgur base 2sprigs of thyme

For the bulgur base 2 tsp vegetable bouillon powder

For the avocado topping 2 tomatoes , cut into wedges

For the avocado topping 4 tbsp chopped basil

For the avocado topping 6 Kalamata olives , halved

For the avocado topping 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil

For the avocado topping 2 tsp cider vinegar

For the avocado topping 2 big handfuls of rocket

For the avocado topping 1 avocado , halved, destoned and chopped

For the beetroot topping 210g can chickpeas , drained

For the beetroot topping 160g cooked beetroot , diced

For the beetroot topping 2 tomatoes , cut into wedges

For the beetroot topping 2 tbsp chopped mint

For the beetroot topping 1 tsp cumin seeds

For the beetroot topping several pinches of ground cinnamon

For the beetroot topping 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil

For the beetroot topping 2 tsp cider vinegar

For the beetroot topping 1 orange , cut into segments

For the beetroot topping 2 tbsp toasted pine nuts

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid
